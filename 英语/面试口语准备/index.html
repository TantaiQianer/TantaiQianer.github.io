自我介绍 (高瓴直博模板)

Good afternoon, esteemed professors. It is my honor to attend this interview.

My name is Li Anyi, and I am currently a junior student at Taishan College, affiliated with Shandong University. Today, I’ll introduce myself through personal quality, scientific research, and future planning.

Firstly, I was selected into Taishan College in my freshman year, which provided me with an exceptional learning environment and unique research experience. I major in computer science and technology, and minor in financial mathematics. I think I have a good foundation in mathematics and logic. That is because I participated in mathematics competitions during high school and twice won provincial first prizes.

Secondly, I have two main research experiences: data visualization and recommendation system. During my data visualization research, I participated in the idea of my paper and helped complete code writing and dataset testing with a senior student. Finally, this item was published at the conference called Visualization, organized by IEEE TVCG and I was the 3rd author. I learned how to translate ideas into project implementation, and my codability improved greatly.

Thirdly, because of my passion for computer science and interests of economics, I make Gaoling my preferred application project. During my doctoral studies, I want to know how to discover feasible ideas and how to pursue the latest research directions. Furthermore, I hope to find a research direction that I can pursue in the long term.

Thank you for your time and consideration.

科研经历

需要说的内容大致包括：

  • 有几项科研经历，分别大致是什么方向的？
  • 对每项科研经历：
    • 研究的是什么领域的什么问题？
    • 做出了哪些贡献
    • 采用了什么关键想法
    • 遇到了哪些困难

I have three research experiences, two of them are about data visualization, the other is about recommendation system.

I think the most important experience is a research on building algorithm of Treemaps. Treemap is a chart to show the proportional relationships by dividing a big rectangle into different size of rectangles.
