# 基本词语

# 关联词

  1. furthermore: 此外

  2. just for completeness sake: 为了完整起见

# 表示程度

  1. remarkably: 非常

    Eg: They still remarkably difficult to train.

  2. to a lesser extent: 在较小程度上

    Eg: This interface has been so successful that modern operating systems—BSD, Linux, macOS, Solaris, and even, to a lesser extent, Microsoft Windows—have Unix-like interfaces.

# 研究思路和过程

  1. heuristically: 探索性地

    Eg: Most current papers dedicated to heuristically finding stable architectures.

# 技术

  1. cutting-edge: 前沿的

    Eg: Being the cutting-edge biologist she is, she utilizes persistent homology to analyze her data and PH reports her data has a prominent cycle, which she further analyzes and is able to confirm that a subset of her 100 genes seem to have a cyclical expression pattern.

  2. novel: 新式的

    Eg: This paper proposes Personalized Diversity-promoting GAN (PD-GAN), a novel recommendation model to generate diverse, yet relevant recommendations.

  3. irreplaceable: 不可替代的

    Eg: Recommender systems have today become irreplaceable in online world to help users filter through a vast amount of information.

  4. paradigm: 样板，范例

    Eg: To learn the first component, we propose a novel pairwise learning paradigm using training pairs.

# 数据和结果

  1. prominent: 显著的，突出的

    Eg: Being the cutting-edge biologist she is, she utilizes persistent homology to analyze her data and PH reports her data has a prominent cycle, which she further analyzes and is able to confirm that a subset of her 100 genes seem to have a cyclical expression pattern.

  2. suboptimal:　不是最优的，欠佳的

    Eg: However, this strategy may often yield suboptimal recommendations.

# 其他

  1. endow: 赋值

    Eg: One can have a function that endows each vertex with a weight.
