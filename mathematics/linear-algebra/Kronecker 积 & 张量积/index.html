# Kronecker 积

Kronecker 积是定义在矩阵上的一种运算。对于两个矩阵 A∈Mm×n(F),B∈Mp×q(F)A \in M_{m \times n}(\mathbb{F}), B \in M_{p \times q}(\mathbb{F})A∈Mm×n​(F),B∈Mp×q​(F), 其克罗内克积 (Kronecker product) 定义为

A⊗B=(a11Ba12B⋯a1nBa21Ba22B⋯a2nB⋮⋮⋱⋮am1Bam2B⋯amnB)∈Mmp×nq(F).A \otimes B = \left(\begin{matrix} a_{11}B & a_{12}B & \cdots & a_{1n}B \\ a_{21}B & a_{22}B & \cdots & a_{2n}B \\ \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\ a_{m1}B & a_{m2}B & \cdots & a_{mn}B \\ \end{matrix}\right) \in M_{mp \times nq}(\mathbb{F}).A⊗B=⎝⎜⎜⎜⎜⎛​a11​Ba21​B⋮am1​B​a12​Ba22​B⋮am2​B​⋯⋯⋱⋯​a1n​Ba2n​B⋮amn​B​⎠⎟⎟⎟⎟⎞​∈Mmp×nq​(F).
